Our Mission

Reinventing spirulina to make it a unique sensory experience, combining nutritional benefits and taste pleasure.

French Excellence

Cultivated near Pau, in southwest France, our spirulina benefits from optimal sunshine and pure water. We have chosen a gentle drying method, at low temperature, to preserve the integrity of this superfood.

Unlike industrial spirulina, Spirulia offers a fine texture and subtle taste, far from the usual standards.
